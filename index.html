Why choose Locci?

Designed from the ground up for developer productivity and maximum performance.

Blazing Fast

Written in Rust and optimized for speed. Locci caches your dependencies and parallelizes your tasks to shave minutes off your build times.

Run Anywhere

Test your CI pipelines exactly as they would run on the cloud, right from your local terminal. No more "push and pray".

Secure by Design

Zero-trust architecture with ephemeral environments. Your secrets never leave your machine unless you explicitly tell them to.
